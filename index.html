About Us

"Evolve is not just a society; it's a movement towards sustainable transportation solutions at MANIT Bhopal. At the forefront of innovation, we pioneer the development of electric vehicles, revolutionizing the way we commute. Recently, we introduced our latest creation: the electric cycle. Combining sleek design with eco-friendly technology, our cycles offer a convenient and environmentally conscious alternative to traditional bikes. Our dedication to pushing boundaries and embracing clean energy reflects a commitment to a brighter, greener future. Through our initiatives and inventions, we inspire the entire MANIT Bhopal community to embrace sustainability and drive positive change."
